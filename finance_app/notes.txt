Goals:
- store stocks in database
- report value/other data from yfinance
- use pandas to report stats on stocks in database https://www.tutorialspoint.com/python_pandas/python_pandas_descriptive_statistics.htm 
- use pandas to visualize stock price change for multiple stocks
